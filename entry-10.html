BUNT

What is a bunt?

A bunt is a technique where the batter taps the ball without a swing. The goal is to get the ball into the infield, very slowly. There are multiple occasions a batter would do this, but the most common occasion is when there is a runner at the bases. This way, the runner can advance to the next base while the batter sacrifices their out. Another occasion is when the batter is a fast runner and is able to run to first base before being ruled out after a bunt. This is usually only possible when the infield positions aren’t prepared for a bunt and are further away.
