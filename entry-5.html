INNINGS

What is an inning?

A typical baseball game is 9 innings long. Each inning has two sides, also called the top and bottom. Each side of the inning has one team batting and the other team playing defense. The away team plays the top of the inning and the home team plays the bottom of the inning. This is a home team advantage, having the last batting chance on the bottom of the 9th inning or being able to finish the game at the top of the 9th inning. Each side of the inning lasts for 3 out counts.
