BASES

What are the different bases?

In a baseball field, there are four total bases. First base, second base, third base, and the home plate.

Stealing Bases

A stolen base is done by the baserunner when they take the next base when they are not entitled to. “This generally occurs when a pitcher is throwing a pitch, but it can also occur while the pitcher still has the ball or is attempting a pickoff, or as the catcher is throwing the ball back to the pitcher.” (MLB Glossary) However, stealing bases can be risky and has its downsides. The baserunner could be ruled out, and statistics show that there is a 50% chance of this happening. Therefore, stealing bases is usually done by fast runners or batters that have a high rate of stolen-base percentage. This also means that it is possible for a baserunner on third base to steal the base to home, which means you can score from stealing bases.
