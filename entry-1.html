STRIKE ZONE

What is a strike zone?

A strike zone is an “area over home plate from the midpoint between a batter’s shoulders and the top of the uniform pants… and a point just below the kneecap.” (MLB Glossary) The strike zone is different for each player based on their height and is measured when the batter is in stance. The strike zone is used to call a pitched ball strike or ball. The pitcher must pitch the ball to through the strike zone for the ball to be called a strike, which are made by the umpires behind the home plate.
