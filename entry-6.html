DOUBLE PLAY

What is a double play?

A double play is when two batters are ruled out within the same play. “Double plays can be made in a number of ways, but the most common form is on a ground ball with a runner on first. In this case, the defense typically throws the ball to second base to get the first out before throwing the ball to the first base for the second out.” (MLB Glossary). A double play is extremely helpful for the defending team. However, it is a major disadvantage for the batting team, as they lost two out counts at once.
