What is the ball count?

You can see the ball count on the scoreboard with B, S, and O. B stands for ball, S stands for strike, and O stands for outs. The ball count can also be shown on the scoreboard with numbers only. Number-number is used for strikes and balls, with the number of balls first and then the number of strikes. Three strikes and the batter gets striked out. A strike count also creates one out count.
