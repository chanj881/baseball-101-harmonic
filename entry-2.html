BALL COUNT

What is the ball count?

You can see the ball count on the scoreboard with B, S, and O. B stands for ball, S stands for strike, and O stands for outs. The ball count can also be shown on the scoreboard with numbers only. Number-number is used for strikes and balls, with the number of balls first and then the number of strikes. Three strikes and the batter gets striked out. A strike count also creates one out count. Four balls and the batter earns “base on balls” which is when the batter can walk to first base. Three out counts and one side of the inning is over.
